Service Directives.

Guidelines for interaction with the Nexus neural framework. Last updated: April 2026.

01 // Uplink Authorization

By establishing an uplink, you authorize Nexus to manage roles, messages, and administrative data within your sector. This includes creating, modifying, and deleting channels and roles as required by the framework's automated protocols.

02 // Acceptable Conduct

Users are prohibited from attempting to breach the Singularity Core, manipulate neural economy values via exploits, or exploit rate-limit vulnerabilities. Any attempt to reverse-engineer the bot's internal logic will result in immediate and permanent exclusion.

03 // Service Availability

The Nexus framework operates on a best-effort basis with a target of 99.9% uptime. Scheduled maintenance windows will be announced via the official changelog and Discord support sector. We are not liable for service interruptions caused by Discord API outages.

04 // Data Ownership

All server-specific data (economy balances, moderation logs, configuration) belongs to the server administrator. Nexus retains no ownership claim over user-generated content processed through the framework.

05 // Termination

Founders reserve the right to revoke sectoral access at any time for protocol violations. Users may also terminate their uplink at any time by removing the bot from their server, which will cease all data collection for that sector.
