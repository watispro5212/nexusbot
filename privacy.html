Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

NexusBot collects minimal data required for its functionality. This include:

  • User IDs: To track economy balances, levels, and warnings.
  • Guild IDs: To store server-specific settings and configurations.
  • Message Content: Only when commands are invoked or for moderation purposes (e.g., purge command). We do not store message history permanently.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected is used solely for:

  • Providing bot features (economy, leveling, etc.).
  • Server moderation and security.
  • Improving bot performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely in local JSON files. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Your Rights

You can request the deletion of your data by contacting the bot owner or by leaving the server where the bot is present (some non-identifiable data may persist until manual cleanup).

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact watispro1.
