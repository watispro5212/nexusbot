## рҹҡҖ The Onboarding Blueprint
*Utilizing Discord's native "Onboarding" feature suite.*

### 1. Welcome Screen
Set up a clean welcome screen with these three focus areas:
1. **Welcome Message**: "Welcome to the Singularity. Establish your uplink in `directives` before proceeding."
2. **Top Channels**: Link to `directives`, `overview`, and `open-ticket`.

### 2. Onboarding Questions
Define these questions in the **Server Settings > Onboarding**:
- **Question 1**: "What is your primary interest?"
  - *Option A*: Core Development (Access to `milestones`)
  - *Option B*: Community (Access to `general-chat`)
  - *Option C*: Technical Support (Access to `support` sector)
- **Question 2**: "Would you like to be notified of broadcasts?"
  - *Yes*: Assign the **Uplink Notifications** role.
